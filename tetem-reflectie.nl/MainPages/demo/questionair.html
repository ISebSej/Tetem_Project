Wie ben ik?

“Who am I” introduces the main theme of “Reflections” by proposing an installation to literally reflect ourselves and upon ourselves. Technology has always mirrored our needs, potentials, aspirations and limits. It is with and through technology that we construct - and at the same time redefine - who we are. With the advent of digital and AI technologies, however, it is more and more complicated to understand what it means to be human. With this installation, we offer a moment to (literally) reflect about technology and how we identify ourselves through it.
